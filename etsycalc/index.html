Etsy Fees & Payments Policy

What do these fees mean?

Transaction fee

6.5% of item price plus shipping charged to customer.

Payment processing

Covers card and payment processing. Default UK model: 4% + £0.20.

Offsite Ads

Charged when Etsy brings the sale from offsite traffic like Google or social ads.

VAT

Applied at 20% on fees, or on fees plus sale value depending on your VAT setting.

Currency conversion

Usually around 2.5% if your payout currency differs from your sale currency.

Fees shown are based on UK Etsy seller fees and may change.
